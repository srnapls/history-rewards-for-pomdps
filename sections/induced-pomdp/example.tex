The induced POMDP corresponding with \defref{d:induced_pomdp} can be seen in \figureref{f:induced_pomdp_example}. If we \texttt{end} the process in state $\langle s_0,n_0\rangle$, $\langle s_1,n_0 \rangle$ or $\langle s_2,n_0 \rangle$ we then obtain a reward of 10, otherwise we obtain a reward of zero.
